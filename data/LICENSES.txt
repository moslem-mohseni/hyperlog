HyLog — Dataset License Attribution
============================================================

HyLog does not redistribute raw datasets. The download_data scripts fetch each archive from its upstream source at run time. The attributions below are required reading before any redistribution.

## HDFS
  upstream: https://github.com/logpai/loghub
  upstream_v2: https://github.com/logpai/loghub-2.0
  citation:
    Xu, W., Huang, L., Fox, A., Patterson, D., & Jordan, M. (2009).
    Detecting Large-Scale System Problems by Mining Console Logs.
    In Proceedings of SOSP 2009.
  redistribution:
    The HDFS dataset is distributed by logpai under research-use terms.
    HyLog does not redistribute the raw archive.

## BGL
  upstream: https://www.usenix.org/cfdr-data
  citation:

    Five System Logs. In Proceedings of DSN 2007.
  redistribution:
    The BGL dataset is distributed by USENIX CFDR for research purposes;
    redistribution within research artifacts is permitted with citation.
